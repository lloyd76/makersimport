Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know you're not a scam?
We provide:
  • Supplier verification reports
  • Order tracking numbers
  • Local pickup verification in Ghana
  • References from past clients
What's your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
It depends on the product:
  • Electronics: Typically 5-10 units
  • Clothing: 20-50 pieces per design
  • Custom items: Case by case basis
Contact us for specific MOQ details.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping to Ghana takes 3-4 weeks by sea (most cost-effective) or 7-10 days by air (more expensive). Exact timing depends on:
  • Product availability
  • Shipping method
  • Customs clearance
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
  • Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo)
  • Bank transfers (Ghanaian banks)
  • Alipay (for funding service)
  • Cash on delivery (limited cases)
